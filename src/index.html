[ 01 // LOCAL_FIRST ]

Data never leaves your machine unless you explicitly export it. Your thoughts are your own. No databases, no tracking, just filesystem-level integrity.

[ 02 // ZERO_CLOUD ]

We don't have servers for your content. We don't have accounts. We don't have a "cloud." We have a binary that runs on your silicon.

[ 03 // KEY_DRIVEN ]

Put the mouse away. Navigate, edit, and organize via a comprehensive command palette and customizable hotkeys. Speed is a requirement, not a feature.

[ 04 // LIGHTWEIGHT ]

Measured in kilobytes, not megabytes. NIL loads instantly and consumes negligible system resources, leaving more room for your ideas to breathe.
